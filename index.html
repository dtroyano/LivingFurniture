All Tables and Bowls are handcrafted from Louisiana woods such as pecan, maple, camphor, magnolia, mulberry and cypress.

These table legs are made by gluing thin strips of wood and bending them, imitating shapes found in Nature.

Tree tables and vine tables can be custom built for your needs. They have a contemporary furniture look and feel and are elegant and functional. These legs are resilient,due to 6 or more laminations and are much stronger than stock wood.

You can feel good knowing that instead of cutting down trees to produce our lumber, we are recycling trees that have already come down.

 Don't throw out that broken chair or table, no matter what side of the family you inherited it or what it has been through.
Chances are furniture can be repaired.
We can do cane and rush as well.
